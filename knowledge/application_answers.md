# Application Answer Templates

Use these only as short wording templates. Canonical facts live in:

- contact/location/notice/relocation: `personal_information.md`
- salary/work authorization/work mode: `job_preferences.md`
- skills/profile/work history: `skills.md`, `professional_profile.md`, `work_experience.md`

## Standard Answers

- Relocation: Yes, open to relocating within India.
- Joining: Immediate. If relocation is required, up to 2 weeks may be needed.
- Expected salary: 6-20 LPA depending on role, responsibilities, and company.
- Experience: Entry level with Fullstack Developer internship experience and production-grade AI/fullstack projects.
- Strongest language: Python.
- Strongest area: Backend engineering and AI systems.
- Frontend: Yes, working experience.
- Backend: Yes, strong working experience.
- Database: Yes, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and SQL.
- Cloud: Basic working knowledge.
- AI experience: Yes, AI agents, RAG, LangChain, LangGraph, and LLM applications.
- Work mode: Remote preferred; open to hybrid and onsite.
